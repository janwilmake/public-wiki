# Thomas Slabbers

**Thomas Slabbers** is a Dutch entrepreneur based in Amsterdam, Netherlands. He is the **Co-founder of [Socialdatabase](https://socialdatabase.com)**, a company specialising in X (Twitter) audience data analysis and targeted advertising, and a formal **X Partner**. He is active on X as [@Thomasslabbers](https://x.com/Thomasslabbers) with 10,900+ followers.

## Career

Slabbers wrote a university research thesis titled *"You are who you follow: A deeper analysis of how people act on Twitter,"* which attracted attention and led to the co-founding of **Socialdatabase** in July 2013 alongside Roland Rijpma and Peter Versteegen.

Socialdatabase analyses publicly available X/Twitter data, processes and recategorises accounts behaviorally, and provides fact-backed audience targeting for advertisers across X, Meta, LinkedIn, and Reddit. The company grew to process **3 billion social accounts** and became an official **X Partner**, giving it direct API access and commercial alignment with the platform. Prior to founding Socialdatabase, Slabbers worked at Twitter in advertising/sales.

He is notably active on X, with posts frequently reaching millions of views on topics related to X platform changes, Dutch media, and AI.
