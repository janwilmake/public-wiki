# Thomas Slabbers

![Thomas Slabbers](https://pbs.twimg.com/profile_images/1837539695093026817/placeholder.jpg)

**Thomas Slabbers** is a Dutch entrepreneur based in Amsterdam, Netherlands. He is the **Co-founder of [Socialdatabase](https://socialdatabase.com)**, a company specialising in X (Twitter) audience data analysis and targeted advertising, and a formal **X Partner**. He is active on X as [@Thomasslabbers](https://x.com/Thomasslabbers) with 10,900+ followers.[^x]

## Career

Slabbers wrote a university research thesis titled *"You are who you follow: A deeper analysis of how people act on Twitter,"* which came to the attention of retired data scientist and rally driver Roland Rijpma. The paper's insights on Twitter data prompted Rijpma's return to professional work, and in **July 2013**, Slabbers, Rijpma, and data engineer Peter Versteegen co-founded **Socialdatabase** in Amsterdam.[^about]

Socialdatabase analyses publicly available X/Twitter data, processes and recategorises accounts behaviorally, and provides fact-backed audience targeting for advertisers across X, Meta, LinkedIn, and Reddit. By 2015, the company's data consumption was significant enough that Twitter invited them to San Francisco HQ for a meeting — where Twitter was impressed by the scale and accuracy of Socialdatabase's database restructuring.[^about]

The company grew to process **3 billion social accounts** and became an official **X Partner**, a status that gives it direct API access and commercial alignment with the platform. Prior to founding Socialdatabase, Slabbers worked at Twitter as an employee in advertising/sales for several years.[^linkedin]

He is notably active on X, with posts frequently reaching millions of views on topics related to X platform changes, Dutch media, and AI.
