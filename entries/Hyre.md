# Hyre

**Hyre** (`hyre.io`) is an AI-powered recruiting platform. [[Jan_Wilmake]] met the founder **Luc van Poll** via Google Meet on **June 10, 2026** to discuss a potential **Founding Engineer** role.[^activity-2026-06-10]

## Company

Hyre provides AI-driven tools for recruitment — including conversation management, candidate pipelines, and integrations with existing hiring workflows. The platform positions itself against competitors like **Metaview** (an AI note-taking tool for interviews) and **Carv**. As of June 2026, Hyre listed both a **Founding Engineer** and a **Fullstack Developer** opening on their careers page.[^activity-2026-06-10]
