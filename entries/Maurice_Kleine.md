# Maurice Kleine

**Maurice Kleine** ([@mauricekleine](https://x.com/mauricekleine)) is a Dutch indie hacker and developer based in Amsterdam. He is the founder of **Mockly** (`getmockly.com`) — a tool that creates mock/prototype data and UI for software development. He is a recurring collaborator and mutual in Jan Wilmake's network, frequently interacting about Cloudflare, MCP, and indie building.[^x-posts-2025-10-06]

## Projects

- **Mockly** (`getmockly.com`) — a mock data and UI prototyping tool that Jan actively supports and upvotes on product launch platforms
- In early 2026, Mockly reached **€700 MRR**, with Maurice aiming for €5,000[^x-posts-2026-01-30]

## Connection to Jan

Jan and Maurice interact frequently on X about:
- Cloudflare Workers and MCP implementation issues (specifically OAuth in Cursor)
- AI tooling and building workflows
- The Amsterdam indie hacker scene (**Hackadam** — recurring hackathon meetups Jan is invited to but often can't attend)

Jan considers Maurice part of his Dutch/Amsterdam builder network. They frequently engage on product launches, code questions, and industry commentary.

## Hackadam

Maurice is an organiser or regular participant in **Hackadam** — a recurring hacker meetup in Amsterdam. Jan was invited to Hackadam sessions in January 2026 and March 2026, though he couldn't always attend. The format: doors open 9:30, intros 10:30, lunch 12:00, demos 16:30, hangout.[^x-bookmarks-2026-03-02]
