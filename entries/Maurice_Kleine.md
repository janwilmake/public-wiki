# Maurice Kleine

**Maurice Kleine** ([@mauricekleine](https://x.com/mauricekleine)) is a Dutch indie hacker and developer based in Amsterdam. He is the founder of **Mockly** (`getmockly.com`) — a tool that creates mock/prototype data and UI for software development. He is a recurring collaborator and mutual in [[Jan_Wilmake]]'s network.

## Connection to Jan

Maurice is part of Jan's Amsterdam developer network. Jan has engaged with him around indie hacker topics, Cloudflare Workers, and developer tooling. He is one of the Dutch developers Jan knows through the local tech community and X interactions.
