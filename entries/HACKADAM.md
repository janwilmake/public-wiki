# HACKADAM

**HACKADAM** 🇳🇱🧀 is a Dutch indie-maker meetup held in Amsterdam, bringing together solo founders, indie hackers, and builders for in-person networking and demos. [[Jan_Wilmake]] was directly tagged for the May 28, 2026 edition by **@AbnerHout** (Abner Hout) on X.

## Format

The typical HACKADAM format: doors open 9:30, intros 10:30, lunch 12:00, demos 16:30, hangout in the evening. It is a recurring event, with previous editions in January 2026 and March 2026.

## May 28, 2026 Edition

On May 22, 2026, @AbnerHout posted the announcement for the May 28, 2026 HACKADAM meetup in Amsterdam, with a follow-up reply directly tagging @janwilmake with *"come come come come!!!!"* — a personal invitation.

Other tagged attendees include [[Maurice_Kleine]], @alecbabaei, @folk_l0rd, @Jonambesa, @JorisFalter, and @borvibe.
