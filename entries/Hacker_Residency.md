# Hacker Residency

**Hacker Residency** (`@HackerResidency`) is a developer co-working retreat that took place in Da Nang, Vietnam in November 2025. It was organized by [[Travis_Fischer]] (`@transitive_bs`) and Phuc Tran Minh (`@phuctm97`), and attended by [[Jan Wilmake]] alongside roughly a dozen other builders and engineers.

## Format and Concept

The residency brought a curated group of developers together to work intensively in a co-located environment for approximately two weeks — shipping projects, giving lightning talks, and collaborating across disciplines. It was sponsored by companies including Stainless, Parallel AI, n8n, and OpenRouter.

The core premise: concentrated time with high-calibre peers accelerates both output and professional relationships in ways that remote-only work cannot replicate.

## Jan's Participation

Jan booked flights to Da Nang in early October 2025, excited to attend alongside `@supernovaio`.[^x-posts-2025-10-09] The residency was held at a location called "The Code Hotel" starting around November 2.[^hrg-timeline]

Jan arrived and participated in standup presentations and lightning talks. His notes from the event describe a high-energy atmosphere: he brought Parallel AI context as his professional angle, explored MCP integrations with attendees, and made connections with developers including:

- **Lilian** — focus sessions and mental health community work
- **Tony** (librechat) — MCP integrations
- A Vietnamese attendee building a food-calorie AI app in Expo

Jan articulated his collaboration potential as: Cloudflare + task automation expertise, with interest in a three-way collaboration structure involving Artem Murzin ([[Artem_Murzin]]) on context management and Andrew (`@EastlondonDev`) on MCP/distributed systems.[^collabs]

## Outcome: Hospitalisation

The residency coincided with the onset of Jan's [[November_2025_Episode]] — a psychosis episode that required hospitalisation. After arriving and working for approximately one week, Jan was hospitalised first at a local Da Nang facility, then transferred to L'Hôpital Français de Hanoi (HFH), before flying home to Groningen via Frankfurt.[^hrg-timeline]

The trip timeline, reconstructed from Jan's notes:[^hrg-timeline]

1. The Code Hotel — arrived November 2; 7–8 nights
2. Attempted Vinmec hospital and fly-out
3. Da Nang psychiatric hospital — local facility, 7–8 days
4. L'Hôpital Français de Hanoi (HFH) — from approximately November 20
5. Flight home via Frankfurt; picked up by his friend Arnoud

Despite the episode, Jan later described the people he encountered during the residency in strongly positive terms: *"I've never been around such an incredibly talented group of people. Shipping with such urgency and passion, all with the best possible vibes."*[^x-posts-2025-11-06]
