# Career Plan 2026

As of early 2026, [[Jan_Wilmake]] is conducting an active job search from [[Groningen]], Netherlands.[^x-posts-2026-03-20] Following the end of his [[Parallel_AI]] contract (November 2025) and a recovery period over December 2025, he entered 2026 seeking stable employment that combines technical depth with AI/LLM work.

## Target Role Profile

Stated preferences:

1. **Daily collaboration** with peers of comparable or greater technical ability
2. **AI/LLM-adjacent work** — API integration, LLM orchestration, context tooling, or agent infrastructure
3. **Location**: Groningen (preferred) or remote-first; Amsterdam acceptable
4. **Company type**: Product company or consultancy with genuine technical depth

Self-described strengths relevant to applications:

- Deep expertise in TypeScript/JavaScript, React Native, Cloudflare Workers
- Rare combination of technical implementation experience with AI/LLM systems
- Track record of building products from zero to paying users independently
- Experience with MCP (Model Context Protocol), OpenAPI ecosystems, and Cloudflare edge infrastructure

## Target Sectors and Companies

### Groningen / Local

Wilmake explored local Groningen opportunities, including IT consultancies and engineering roles aligned with his TypeScript and AI background.

### API / LLM Orchestration Space

Notable target categories include API integration platforms, LLM routing, agent tooling, and developer-tooling companies — areas that map directly onto his work on [[uithub]], [[ContextArea]], and [[installthismcp]].

### Remote-First / Amsterdam

A broad set of remote-first and Amsterdam-based startups were evaluated throughout the spring, with a particular focus on AI engineer and full-stack product engineer roles.

## Networking Strategy

Networking channels used during the search:

- **In-person events** — AI Tinkerers, Internet Friends, and the [[HACKADAM]] indie-maker meetup in Amsterdam
- **Local meetups** — aiGrunn Café in Groningen
- **X (Twitter)** as @janwilmake for professional networking and product announcements

## Public Announcement

On March 20, 2026, Jan publicly announced his job search on X: *"Hi! Based in the Netherlands, I'm looking for a new opportunity as full-stack product engineer with experience with React (Native), Node.js and Cloudflare, and generally, building entire products from scratch. DMs open!"*[^x-posts-2026-03-20]

## Outcome: Joined Hyre

By early July 2026, the search concluded with Jan signing a freelance (ZZP) contract with [[Hyre]] (`hyre.io`) as a founding engineer, where his work focuses on Model Context Protocol (MCP) tooling for recruitment workflows. The progression from discovery to signed contract took under four weeks — the fastest cycle of the entire search.[^x-posts-2026-07-09]
