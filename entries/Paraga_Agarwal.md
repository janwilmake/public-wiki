# Paraga Agarwal

**Paraga Agarwal** (`@paraga`) is an entrepreneur and technology executive best known as the former CEO of Twitter (now X). He is the founder and CEO of **[[Parallel_AI]]** — a San Francisco-based AI company building next-generation web search and retrieval infrastructure for AI agents. [[Jan_Wilmake]] worked directly under him as a DevRel contractor from August to November 2025.

## Background

Prior to Parallel AI, Paraga served as CEO of Twitter following Jack Dorsey's departure. He is described by Jan as leading an *"incredibly talent-dense team who are rebuilding web infrastructure for AI from first principles."*

## Parallel AI

Under Paraga's leadership, Parallel AI focused on:

- **Web Search API** — state-of-the-art web search engineered for AI agents, with proprietary index and retrieval infrastructure
- **Extract API** — HTML-to-markdown extraction with configurable engines
- **Task API** — long-running parallel research tasks with multiple processor tiers
- **Monitor API** — always-on web monitoring with structured output schemas

In November 2025, Paraga shared benchmark results showing Parallel Search outperforming products built over years by major companies.

In January 2026, Parallel's web search was made available in preview on **Google Vertex AI**.

## Connection to Jan

Jan publicly announced his Parallel AI role on August 6, 2025, citing Paraga directly: *"Very excited and grateful to be working with @paraga (ex-Twitter CEO) and his incredibly talent-dense team."*
