# Ives van Hoorne

**Ives van Hoorne** (`@CompuIves` on X) is a Dutch software engineer and entrepreneur, best known as the creator and co-founder of **CodeSandbox** — one of the most widely-used online code editors and development environments. He is also the creator of [[Biscuit]], an all-in-one business-building platform.[^activity-2026-05-12]

## Career

### CodeSandbox

CodeSandbox is an online IDE that allows developers to create, share, and collaborate on web applications directly in the browser. It became one of the most prominent tools in the JavaScript/React ecosystem and raised significant venture capital funding. Van Hoorne is one of the most recognised Dutch tech founders in the developer tools space.

### Biscuit

Van Hoorne's current project is [[Biscuit]] (`biscuit.so`), described as "the all-in-one platform for building your business." Biscuit targets solo builders and founders, combining project management, assets, data models, brand identity, email, workflows, and preview capabilities. The company has an office in Amsterdam with canal views, actively hosts builder events, and is hiring.[^browser-2026-05-12]

His company email domain is `flits.inc`.[^browser-2026-05-04]

Following the meeting, **Maria Martin** from Biscuit emailed Jan on May 13, 2026 about the Publican project — suggesting the meeting opened a substantive relationship between Jan and the Biscuit team.[^activity-2026-05-13]
