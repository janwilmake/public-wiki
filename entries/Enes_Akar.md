# Enes Akar

![Enes Akar](https://github.com/enesakar.png)

**Enes Akar** is a Turkish-American software engineer and entrepreneur based in San Jose / Palo Alto, California. He is the **Co-founder and CEO of [Upstash](https://upstash.com)**, a serverless data platform offering Redis, Kafka, QStash, and Vector databases optimised for edge and serverless environments. He is active on X as [@enesakar](https://x.com/enesakar) and also co-founded **Context7.ai**.[^x]

## Career

Akar holds a BS in Computer Engineering and an MBA from **Boğaziçi University** (Istanbul, Turkey). He has 10+ years of experience in distributed systems. Prior to Upstash, he was **CTO of Hazelcast Cloud** (August 2018 – October 2020) and worked at **Confluent**, the Kafka-as-a-service company.[^auth0]

He co-founded **Upstash** in 2021, building it into the leading serverless-first data platform for edge runtimes (Cloudflare Workers, Vercel Edge, Deno Deploy). Upstash raised **$10M from Andreessen Horowitz (a16z)** and is known for its HTTP-first API design that works natively without persistent connections — critical for serverless functions.[^story]

Upstash's product suite includes:
- **Upstash Redis** — serverless Redis with per-request pricing
- **Upstash Kafka** — deprecated 2025 in favour of Upstash Workflow
- **QStash** — serverless message queue / task scheduling
- **Upstash Vector** — serverless vector database for AI embeddings

In 2025–2026, Akar also co-founded **Context7.ai**, a context-layer tool for AI applications.
