# Groningen

![Grote Markt and Martinitoren, Groningen](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/3/3d/20100523_Grote_Markt_en_Martinitoren_Groningen_NL.jpg)

## University of Groningen (RUG)

The **Rijksuniversiteit Groningen** (RUG), founded in 1614, is one of the Netherlands' oldest universities.[^2015-2022] Wilmake completed his Bachelor of Science in Artificial Intelligence there between approximately 2012 and 2016. In 2025, he returned in a supervisory capacity, overseeing four software engineering students working on [[uithub]]-related tooling.[^2024-2025]
