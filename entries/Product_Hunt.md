# Product Hunt

**Product Hunt** is an online community and daily leaderboard for new technology products, founded by [[Ryan_Hoover]] in November 2013 as a simple email digest of the day's best new products. It quickly became the primary launch platform for developer tools, SaaS products, and consumer apps.

## History

Product Hunt launched as a side project before growing into the leading discovery platform for startups and indie developers. It was **acquired by AngelList in 2016** and Hoover stepped down as CEO in October 2020. Despite the acquisition, it has remained the default launch venue for most developer-facing products. Ben Tossell ([[Ben_Tossell]]) worked at Product Hunt as one of the first hires in 2015, running community and launches.
