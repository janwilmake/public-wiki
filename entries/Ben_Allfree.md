# Ben Allfree

**Ben Allfree** ([@benallfree](https://x.com/benallfree)) is a developer and entrepreneur who appears regularly in [[Jan_Wilmake]]'s X network. He is one of Jan's closest X mutuals.

## Background

Ben is a prolific open-source developer with a background that includes a startup exit and work in conservation. He is active in the Cloudflare Workers and Durable Objects community.

Jan and Ben interact on X around technical topics including MCP, agent infrastructure, Cloudflare Workers, and indie builder culture.

Jan credits X as a platform that gave him access to a professional network he would not have built otherwise, and lists Ben among the people central to that network.
