# Ben Allfree

**Ben Allfree** ([@benallfree](https://x.com/benallfree)) is a developer and entrepreneur who appears regularly in Jan Wilmake's X network. He is one of Jan's five closest X mutuals, identified by Jan alongside Sam Goodwin, Matt Carey, EastlondonDev, and Artem Murzin as people he values highly.[^x-posts-2025-10-06]

## Background

Ben is notably more accomplished than his casual X presence suggests. Jan's AI-powered partnership analysis revealed him to be a **$35M exit veteran**, a conservation leader, and a musician — a profile the two had never discussed directly in their X interactions.[^collabs-2025]

## Connection to Jan

Jan and Ben interact on X around technical topics including MCP, agent infrastructure, Cloudflare Workers, and indie builder culture. Jan lists Ben among the people he would not have known without X, which he credits as the foundation of his professional network.[^x-posts-2025-10-06]

In October 2025, Jan used the Parallel Task API to do a *"partnership analysis"* of his top five X contacts — including Ben — using AI research to find surprising information about each person that hadn't been discussed directly in their interactions. A specific collaboration area identified: co-authoring a proposal for Cloudflare to implement pluggable filesystem architecture based on Ben's work on Durable Objects filesystem standards.[^x-posts-2025-10-09][^collabs-2025]
