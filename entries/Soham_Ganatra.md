# Soham Ganatra

![Soham Ganatra](https://github.com/GanatraSoham.png)

**Soham Ganatra** (born c. 1997) is an Indian entrepreneur based in San Francisco. He is the **Co-founder and CEO of [Composio](https://composio.dev)**, a developer infrastructure platform that enables AI agents to reliably integrate with external tools and services. He is active on X as [@GanatraSoham](https://x.com/GanatraSoham) and was named to the **Forbes India 30 Under 30, Class of 2026**.

## Career

Ganatra holds a background from the **Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay (IIT-B)**. Before founding Composio, he worked as a product and engineering manager at a startup, where he observed developers spending roughly a third of engineering time on tedious systems integrations.

He founded **Composio** in 2023. After an early pivot, Composio found traction by building a **tool-calling infrastructure layer**: 250+ pre-built integrations (GitHub, Salesforce, Outlook, etc.) with high accuracy for AI agent use cases.

Growth milestones:

- Viral tweet by CrewAI founder drove first significant user wave
- Scaled from 1,000 to **100,000 developers** within approximately a year
- Raised **$30M Series A from Lightspeed**
- 200,000+ developers, 100M+ tool calls/month as of 2026
