# EastlondonDev

**EastlondonDev** ([@EastlondonDev](https://x.com/EastlondonDev)) is a London-based software developer and entrepreneur. He has 15+ years of software engineering experience, has written a small O'Reilly book, served as CTO and founder, and raised over $30M with a startup called justbobsledit.[^x-posts-2026-01-19]

## Projects

He is known in the AI developer community for:

- **vibe-tools** — a context management and tool-use layer for AI coding assistants, used alongside Conare
- **mineflare** — a Cloudflare Workers-based project deployed with the Alchemy framework (GitHub: `eastlondoner/mineflare`)
- Contracting at **Replit** as SRE during this period

## Connection to Jan

Jan and EastlondonDev interact regularly on X about MCP, Durable Objects, agent patterns, and vibe-coding. EastlondonDev publicly noted in October 2025 that he uses Conare for full vibe-coding and Cursor for AI-assisted coding — and that he sees Conare as *"an awesome IDE for Claude Code which lets me easily manage a bunch of Claude Code instances in different contexts."*[^x-posts-2025-10-28]

He participated in the xAI Hackathon in London (January 2026) alongside Jan's contact Artem Murzin, where their team won first place with *"Grok runs for Mayor of London."*[^x-posts-2026-01-19]

Jan listed EastlondonDev as one of five core X relationships he values, alongside Sam Goodwin, Ben Allfree, Matt Carey, and Artem Murzin.[^x-posts-2025-10-06]
