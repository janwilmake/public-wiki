# EastlondonDev

**EastlondonDev** ([@EastlondonDev](https://x.com/EastlondonDev)) is a London-based software developer and entrepreneur. He has 15+ years of software engineering experience, has authored a small O'Reilly book, served as CTO and founder, and raised over $30M with a startup.

## Projects

He is known in the AI developer community for:

- **vibe-tools** — a context management and tool-use layer for AI coding assistants, used alongside Conare
- **mineflare** — a Cloudflare Workers-based project deployed with the Alchemy framework

## Community

EastlondonDev publicly noted in October 2025 that he sees Conare (by [[Artem_Murzin]]) as *"an awesome IDE for Claude Code which lets me easily manage a bunch of Claude Code instances in different contexts."*

He participated in the xAI Hackathon in London (January 2026) alongside Artem Murzin, where their team won first place with *"Grok runs for Mayor of London."*

## Connection to Jan

[[Jan_Wilmake]] lists EastlondonDev as one of his core X relationships, alongside Sam Goodwin, Ben Allfree, Matt Carey, and Artem Murzin. Jan and EastlondonDev interact on X about MCP, Durable Objects, agent patterns, and vibe-coding.
